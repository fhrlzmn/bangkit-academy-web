Improve your skills with Bangkit

What is Bangkit?

The Bangkit program, which is led by Google, GoTo, and Traveloka, is a career readiness initiative that was launched in 2020 as part of the Kampus Merdeka program. It offers three different learning paths, namely machine learning, mobile development, and cloud computing, and is designed to provide students with in-demand skills and tech certifications. In addition to teaching technical skills, Bangkit also focuses on developing soft skills and English proficiency to prepare students for their transition from academia to the workplace. By completing the program, you'll be equipped with the expertise and skills necessary to succeed at leading companies.

Learning Paths

In Bangkit, you have the option to choose from three different learning paths.

Machine Learning

Learn about AI's basic concepts and applications so that you can solve a wide variety of problems in Machine Learning.

Cloud Computing

Learn the basics of cloud computing to successfully manage and deploy applications, monitor operations, and handle enterprise solutions.

Mobile Development

Discover the essential concepts and skills required to start your career as a professional Android developer.
